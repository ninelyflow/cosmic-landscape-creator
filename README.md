# Cosmic Landscape Creator — Variable Prompt System

**One master prompt. 10^5 worlds. Zero creative block.**

A Midjourney prompt engine that generates thousands of unique cosmic landscapes by swapping variables across 5 categories — Nebula Color, Mountain Type, Water Body, Atmosphere, and Art Style.

## How It Works

```
/imagine [pick a nebula] swirling over [pick mountains] with [pick water] at [pick atmosphere], [pick a style], cosmic landscape --ar 16:9 --v 6
```

Each category has 10 options. That's 10 × 10 × 10 × 10 × 10 = **100,000 unique combinations**. You'll never generate the same landscape twice.
